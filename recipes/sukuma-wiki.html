Description

Sukuma Wiki (collard greens) is one of the most common vegetable dishes in Kenya. The name means “stretch the week” because it’s affordable and eaten often.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (collard greens), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add sukuma wiki, stir, and cook for 5–7 minutes until tender but still green.
  4. Season with salt and serve hot with ugali.
