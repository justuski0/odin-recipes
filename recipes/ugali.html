Ugali

Home

Description

Ugali is Kenya’s staple dish, made by cooking maize flour with water until it forms a stiff porridge. It’s usually served as an accompaniment to vegetables, meat, or fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize (corn) flour
  • 4 cups water
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Add maize flour gradually while stirring with a wooden spoon.
  3. Keep stirring and pressing to remove lumps until thick.
  4. Cover and steam on low heat for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with sukuma wiki, stew, or nyama choma.
