Description

Nyama Choma (roasted meat) is Kenyaâ€™s unofficial national dish. Goat or beef is marinated and roasted over an open flame, often enjoyed with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 2 kg goat or beef meat (on the bone preferred)
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Pepper or chili (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into large chunks.
  2. Season with salt and optional spices or lemon juice.
  3. Grill slowly over hot charcoal until browned outside and tender inside.
  4. Chop into bite-sized pieces and serve with kachumbari (fresh tomato and onion salad).
